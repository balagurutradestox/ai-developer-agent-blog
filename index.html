How It Works The Magic Flow Explained

The magic happens through a beautifully simple workflow. It starts with a conversation ‚Äî you tell the AI what you need, whether it's a new feature, a bug fix, or refactoring existing code. The agent analyzes your entire codebase, understanding the relationships between different components, the patterns you've established, and the goals you're working toward.

Based on this understanding, the AI creates a plan, writes the code, and submits it as a pull request for your review. Everything is transparent ‚Äî you see every change, every decision, and can provide feedback at any step. It's not about replacing developers; it's about augmenting our capabilities and freeing us to focus on the interesting problems.
